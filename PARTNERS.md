# FadeJunkie — Partners

> Defines the partnership program, partner types, and intake process.
> The Partners page at `/partners` is built from this doc.

---

## What Partnerships Mean at FadeJunkie

FadeJunkie partners are organizations that serve the barber industry —
schools, distributors, tool brands, platform companies. Partnerships are
peer-to-peer: we bring reach and tools, partners bring audience and credibility.

We do not do pay-to-play partnerships. If someone is paying to be featured,
that's advertising, not a partnership.

---

## Partner Types

### 🎓 Barber Schools
**What we offer:**
- Group Mode access for live classroom study sessions
- Co-branded study materials (future)
- Featured school listing in the FadeJunkie directory (Phase 4)

**What we ask:**
- Recommend FadeJunkie to students
- Share feedback on curriculum gaps
- Pilot new features with their cohorts

**Current status:** Actively seeking first school partners in Texas.

---

### 🛠 Tool & Product Brands
**What we offer:**
- Brand placement in the Education Hub (tools topic questions reference real products)
- Merch collab opportunities
- Audience access — barber students and working pros

**What we ask:**
- Product samples or trial access for research
- Educational content contribution (tool guides, technique resources)

**Current status:** Open to outreach.

---

### 📦 Distributors
**What we offer:**
- Co-marketing to barber student audience
- Integration into future barber economy tools

**What we ask:**
- Student pricing programs
- Distribution network for FadeJunkie merch (future)

**Current status:** Planning phase.

---

### 🔗 Platform Partners
**What we offer:**
- API integration into future FadeJunkie platform
- Co-branded study or booking tools

**What we ask:**
- Data sharing (anonymized, aggregate) for industry insights
- Revenue share on referred barbers

**Current status:** Phase 4 discussion.

## Intake Process

1. Partner reaches out to `partners@fadejunkie.com`
2. Anthony reviews — response within 5 business days
3. Intro call (30 min) to align on value exchange
4. Agreement defined (no templates yet — each is custom)
5. Partner listed on `/partners` page once active

---

## What Makes a Good Partner

- Serves barbers or barber students directly
- Values the culture — not looking to extract from it
- Willing to engage authentically, not just transactionally
- Understands FadeJunkie is a brand, not just a lead source

---

## What We Won't Do

- Paid placement disguised as partnership
- Partnerships with brands that compete with barbers (e.g., chains undermining booth-rent culture)
- Any arrangement that requires compromising study content objectivity
- Exclusive partnerships that lock out competitor tools if those tools serve barbers better
